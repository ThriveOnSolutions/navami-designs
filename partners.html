How we vet

Trades that stay.

Most builders rotate trades to chase the lowest bid. We don't. The same plumber who roughed Grant Ave is roughing Zambrano. Continuity is quality.

01

License + insurance verified

TDLR / TREC / TX RCAT pulled annually. COI on file before the first day on site.

02

Reference walk-through

We visit one of their last three jobs. We talk to the owner without the trade in the room.

03

Net-15 payment

No 60-day floats. Subs paid in two weeks, not two seasons. AI ledger triggers the wire.

04

Public credit at close

Every project page on the site lists the trades. Every Zillow listing gets the same.
