Method · 6 principles

How we build.

Guaranteed Maximum Price. Owner-accessible ledger. Net-15 subcontractor pay. Lifetime-cost decisions. AI-managed paperwork. Long-horizon design.

01

GMP. Underruns refunded.

Guaranteed Maximum Price contracts. If the project closes under cap — duplicate orders caught, framing under quote, sub overestimates — the delta is refunded to the owner at closing. Not retained as builder contingency.
02

Live owner ledger.

Day-one client login. Every quote, invoice, change order, and rebate logged in real time. Sortable by trade, phase, or vendor. CPA-ready export. AI Q&A on cost movements.
03

Lifetime-cost decisions.

Bids selected on 10-year cost-of-ownership, not lowest sticker. Example: $40K HVAC with 20-year service life selected over $22K unit with 7-year service life. AI runs lifetime math; owner approves.
04

Subs named & paid net-15.

All GCs and trades listed publicly on Partners page. Payment terms: net-15 or better. License + insurance verified annually. Written reference at project close.
05

AI handles paperwork.

City permits, HOA covenants, COI tracking, lender draw packets, insurance riders, inspection scheduling — automated. Human judgment reserved for design, finishes, and site walks.
06

10-year design horizon.

Features must justify themselves over 10 years of daily use. Storage right-sized, sightlines tested in real daylight, acoustics scoped at the framing stage. No magazine-only details.
