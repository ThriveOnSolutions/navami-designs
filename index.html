FAQ · 06

Common questions.

Don't see what you're looking for? Reserve a discovery call — 48-hour response.

Q1What does Navami Designs build?

Custom single-family homes (4–5 bed, 3,300–4,500 sqft, $1.2M–$2.5M) in Alamo Heights, Terrell Hills, Olmos Park, and Mahncke Park. Also commercial: retail, restaurant, medical, office, mixed-use ($250K–$6M+).

Q2How long does a custom home take?

Cottage (2,000–2,800 sqft): 4–6 months. Estate (3,000–4,500 sqft): 8–11 months. Bespoke (architect-led): 12–18 months. Schedule slip risk is tracked weekly in the owner platform.

Q3What is a GMP contract?

Guaranteed Maximum Price — a fixed-cap construction contract. Underruns are refunded to the owner at closing. Navami does not retain underrun savings as builder margin.

Q4Where do you build?

Primarily Bexar County: Alamo Heights (78209), Terrell Hills, Olmos Park, Mahncke Park. Commercial work extends to Boerne and New Braunfels.

Q5Do you build on land I already own?

Yes. We also source lots — 2 in Olmos Park and 1 in Mahncke Park are currently optioned for 2026 starts.

Q6How is the AI platform used?

Real-time budget ledger, permit/HOA tracking, COI vault, mortgage draw packet compilation, subcontractor scheduling, GMP underrun calculator. Owner access included 2 years post-close.
